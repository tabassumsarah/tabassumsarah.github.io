Find Me

Hi! My name is Sarah Tabassum. Every time I mention my name I have to use my full name because there is always at least another sara or sarah in the room :) I have been planning to start my own blog for about 5+ years or more. Yes so what took this long? I would not lie that I tried, If you search you can find couple of my unfinished blogs with half-baked posts in the Internet world. Power of procrastination is so mighty that I got defeated every time. But hey, haven't you read Robert the Bruce and the spider?
Recent days I have started to get involved in tech communities more than before. And every time I see talented people with full of energy and dedication. I am getting inspired more and more to be honest. Speaking of which I should mention that this played a big role in spending a handful of my time to structure this blog. Nothing fancy to brag about, I decided to use this blog as a medium of accountability towards my goals and wish lists.
Currently I live in Sydney, a beautiful city in NSW, Australia. Moving to sydney was a big change in my life. I gained immense amount of lessons in this transition where some of those are postive and some are debatable :p I occasionaly write about random take on my experience and overall life style that I built for myself so far from my home town.
By profession I am a software developer. You will find a section where I document all my learning’s and findings bout tech and programming. I love photography. I used to be more regular, participating on photo walks and safaris. Not that regular anymore still whenever I go on vacations I try to spend a little bit of time in taking photos. Here are my flickr and instagram incase if you would like to checkout some amateur photographs. I am a bit of restless soul, try to do lots of things at once and fail miserably! I love to draw and paint. Took some lessons last year and this year on watercolor and drawing and loved every bit of it.
I am a vivid sleeper. I am not sure if anyone ever used 'vivid' and 'sleeper' together but it’s true. Most of the time when I should be doing something meaningful I tend to sleep. I am working on that. I have two beautiful cats Tj and Cricket. These two amazing fellas keep me warm and happy when I go home after a long day. Not because they are friendly and cuddly but mostly because I am cuddly and friendly towards them!
I love star wars and greys anatomy and not ashamed to mention those two in the same go. Silent Hill is one of my favorite movies. I love colors around me. Here you can look into my desk at work and desk at home! I love reading no doubt but as I said my sleeping ritual often comes in between! I am having a section where I will post what I am currently reading.
My motto in life is ‘Everyday struggle to be better than yesterday!’. It may sound little bit cheesy but that’s the story of me! You can find source code for this blog on my github profile.
